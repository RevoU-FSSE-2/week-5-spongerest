Vision

We aim to make everyone love books and we shall develop readers by making books more accessible to the people who need them the most. Our purpose is to serve our customers with tools that enrich their minds and improve skills to ensure their success. All our efforts are concentrated to serve this purpose.

Mission

To provide quality but affordable books for education, entertainment, self-development and self-fulfillment

Based

PASSION. ADVENTURE. INTEGRITY.

Halsion Books started with a dream to give every book lover the same opportunity to fulfill their reading needs. "Halsion" comes from the word "Halcyon" in English which means calm, peaceful, just like if you reading a book in a calm state, it will be very pleasant, so I go with the word "Halsion" eventually for more-Indonesian version.

The books we offer always emphasize the basic principles of Halsion Books, namely Price, Quality, and Readers' Needs. Halsion Books works closely with local book readers, other bookstores, and distributors to obtain used book products of a good standard and that don't disturb the reader's comfort.

Our passion leads to innovation in products, processes and business models. Utilize superior resources, preloved books search techniques, and customized selection of preloved books to get premium quality.
